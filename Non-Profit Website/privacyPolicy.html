Privacy Policy

Learn about our commitment to data privacy and protection.

Our Approach to Privacy

We take data privacy and protection seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. We comply with all applicable data protection laws and regulations to ensure your data is handled securely.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide to us, such as your name, email address, phone number, and address. We may also collect information about your interactions with our website and services, including IP addresses and cookies.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to provide services, communicate with you, process donations, and improve our offerings. We may also use your information for internal analytics and marketing purposes, but we will never sell your data to third parties.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also opt out of receiving marketing communications from us. Contact us if you have any questions or requests regarding your personal data.

Animal Care Allies

Animal Care Allies is dedicated to the well-being and protection of all creatures, great and small. Through advocacy, education, and community outreach, we strive to create a world where every animal is treated with compassion and respect. Join us in our mission to be the voice for those who cannot speak for themselves.
